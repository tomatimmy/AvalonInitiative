AVALON

Creating an Ideal Future with Permaculture

Avalon is a property in Costa Rica where permaculture principles of earth care, people care, and fair share are at the forefront of everything we do. Avalon is surrounded by indigenous reserves, offering unique cultural experiences and a pristine jungle teeming with wildlife. We’re also just a short distance from Puerto Viejo and Cahuita, two laid-back Caribbean towns with gorgeous beaches and a diverse cultural and culinary scene.

At Avalon, we are committed to making a positive impact on the environment, the people around us, and the world. Our mission is to create a regenerative permaculture homestead that not only nurtures the community and regenerates the land, but also creates a space for people to reconnect with themselves, connect with nature, and the local community.

We value collaboration and sharing over competition and exploitation. That's why we're committed to improving sustainability and eco-tourism in the area and redirecting the flow of tourism money to the local community. Especially to the indigenous zone, inhabited by the Bribri, Cabécar, and Ngäbe-Buglé, three of the eight indigenous peoples of Costa Rica.

We value different knowledge systems and combine them to make them available to everyone interested. When working the land, for example, we connect indigenous wisdom with modern knowledge, and when making business decisions, we consider the culture of all people involved.

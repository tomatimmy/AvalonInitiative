PERMACULTURE: A WAY OF LIVING

We are creating our version of the ideal future by applying permaculture principles to building a life and a business while connecting with nature, with like-minded people from all over the world, and the local community.

Whether you're seeking a new adventure, a possibility to make a positive impact, or looking to reset, rethink, and redesign your life, Avalon is the perfect place for you. Join us as we create a new reality for ourselves and others, one that is rooted in permaculture principles and values.

Welcome to Avalon, where the impossible becomes possible, and the extraordinary is the norm.

Invest in the Avalon Initiative

HELP US BUILD A BETTER FUTURE

Our next initiatives are focused on creating a self-sustaining food forest on a former pasture. This ecosystem will improve soil structure and fertility and produce food and other organic products without requiring excessive water, fertilizer, or labor inputs. We will also implement a well and water management system to conserve and optimize rainwater usage. Furthermore, we will enforce a gray water system that recycles and reuses wastewater without chemical treatment.

Join us in creating a more sustainable and equitable world! You can support specific initiatives through Patreon, or get in touch with us to discuss site visits or explore other investment opportunities.
